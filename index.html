Strum Media is a Melbourne-based media production and social media management company servicing construction, property, infrastructure and corporate businesses Australia-wide. We specialise in construction videography, commercial photography, drone and FPV filming, project content, and ongoing social strategy and management — helping builders, developers and commercial brands showcase projects, document site progress, and create high-quality content for websites, Instagram, LinkedIn, tenders and campaigns.
